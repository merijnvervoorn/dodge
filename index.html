Welcome to the Department of Dutch Government Efficiency

At DODGE, we are committed to modernizing government operations to ensure greater efficiency, productivity, and value for Dutch citizens. Our mission is to streamline governmental processes through advanced technology and innovative solutions.

Learn More About Us

Our Purpose

The Department of Dutch Government Efficiency (DODGE) was established to implement the Prime Minister's Efficiency Agenda. Our goal is to maximize efficiency in government operations, leveraging modern technology and best practices to create a more responsive, transparent, and accountable government.

Key Initiatives

  • Improving software and IT infrastructure across all government agencies
  • Promoting interoperability and data synchronization between government systems
  • Enhancing data protection and privacy measures
  • Reducing bureaucratic inefficiencies through process automation
  • Providing training and resources for government employees to adapt to new technologies
