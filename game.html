EFFISHNT: The Firing AI

In this game, you are a prototype AI tool called EFFISHNT. This tool is being tested by the HR manager of Optimyze, a company that creates digital tools for public institutions. EFFISHNT is secretly used internally by the manager of HR to decide who to fire in search of efficiency within the company.

Why this game?

This game serves as a commentary on the use of AI in decision-making, particularly in the context of employee evaluations and efficiency-driven agendas. It highlights the potential pitfalls of relying on technology to make complex, human-centered decisions like firing employees based on arbitrary performance metrics.

Created as a critique of modernizing government operations, the game questions the effectiveness of using automated tools to solve intricate human problems. It invites players to reflect on the challenges of balancing efficiency, fairness, and empathy in organizational decision-making.
