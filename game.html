Why this game?

This game serves as a commentary on the use of AI in employee evaluations and efficiency-driven agendas. The DOGE (Department of Government Efficiency) is allegedly attempting to implement AI to assess the performance of government workers, and this game explores why that could lead to significant problems. It highlights the dangers of relying on technology to make complex, human-centered decisions, such as firing employees based on arbitrary performance metrics.

It invites players to reflect on the challenges of balancing efficiency, fairness, and empathy in decision-making within organizations. The game emphasizes that a simple list of 5 bullet points is insufficient to evaluate an employee's true value to a business. Such important decisions should not be left to AI.
